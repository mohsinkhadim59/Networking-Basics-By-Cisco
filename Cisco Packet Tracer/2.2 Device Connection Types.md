- There multiple type of connection cables present in the bottom left corner of the Cisco Packet Tracer when you click on the connection like :
	- Automatically Choose Connection Type
	- Copper Straight Through
	- Console
	- Copper Crossover
- You can select the Copper Straight Through cable to connect the **Switch** and **PC** together just like below and select any ethernet ports you want to connect on both of them (in case having multiple ports in case of a switch).

- Switches can be connected to each other using cross over cable as the rule is to connect two light devices using cross over cable but it can vary depending upon the device and port.
![Swtiches Connection](/Images/Switches.png)

>[!tip]
>You might see orange color instead of green when making connections via cables this is because the negotiation between devices hasn't happened yet. You can press the fast forward button in the bottom left to make it quickly happen so that you may the green lights on to ensure your connection is good.
