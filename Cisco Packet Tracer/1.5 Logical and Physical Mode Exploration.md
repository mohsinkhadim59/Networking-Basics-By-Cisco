>[!Notes] **Objectives**
>Objectives of this activity are :
>- Investigate Devices in a Wiring Closet
>- Connect End Devices to Networking Devices
>- Install a Backup Router
>- Configure a Hostname

>[!Notes]
>You can inspect the devices in the **Physical Mode** and then zoom in and **Right-Click** the device you want to explore, menu will appear where you can click the **Inspect Front or Inspect Rear** to closely see the ports and consoles of the devices and it can also ease in connecting the devices with the cables to your desired ports.
